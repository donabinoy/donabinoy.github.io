About Me

I am a detail-oriented and results-driven Senior Software Engineer with over 6 years of experience specializing in native iOS application development. My expertise lies in building, optimizing, and maintaining complex applications using Swift, SwiftUI, and UIKit. I have a proven track record of integrating with hardware via Bluetooth (BLE) for consumer electronics and developing secure, HIPAA-compliant solutions for the healthcare industry. As a collaborative team player with a strong foundation in software architecture, I am passionate about creating modular, reusable, and maintainable code, frequently utilizing the Swift Package Manager to achieve this.
